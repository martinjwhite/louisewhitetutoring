Making Sense of Letters and Numbers

Experienced, Personalised Primary Tuition

With over 30 years of experience as a primary teacher, I bring a deep passion for helping children develop confidence and success in reading, writing, and maths.

My years of teaching in primary schools have shown me that some children need more time than the classroom can always provide. When children move on before fully understanding a concept, this can affect confidence and progress. I help identify gaps in learning and build bespoke programmes that close those gaps and support lasting confidence.

📚

Areas of Support

  • Specialist phonics teaching across EYFS and KS1.
  • Support to close learning gaps and prepare for the Year 1 Phonics Screening Check.
  • Tailored early maths and KS2 learning support.
  • Sessions adapted to each child's individual needs.
🤝

Additional Needs and Nurture Approach

Working within an accredited nurture school, I have a strong background in supporting children with additional needs and EHCPs in a calm, trauma-informed, and nurturing way.

I have experience working with children with specific learning difficulties, sensory needs, anxiety, social and communication differences, as well as dyslexia, ADHD, and ASD.

🧠

How Sessions Work

My approach is gentle, supportive, and personalised. Sessions include brain breaks and confidence-building activities to help every child feel safe, motivated, and ready to learn.

I offer daytime, evening, and weekend sessions, and I am available during school holidays.
